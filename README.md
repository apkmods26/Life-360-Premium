
Which outstanding application does your family use to check each other’s safety level? If you can’t find it, you can immediately use Life360: Live Location Sharing to share the safe location of members. This is an application that improves safety and is used by many people around the world. It also has many new features to help you connect with your loved ones easily.

REAL-TIME LOCATION SHARING
One of the attractive features is real-time sharing for your relatives and friends. This feature helps family members share their current location, helping parents monitor their children or detect abnormalities if a signal is transmitted.

Peace of mind: Members with this application can turn on GPS to share each other’s location in real-time. This helps you know what positions your relatives, friends or people you love are in.
Kid tracking: This feature is really suitable for parents who want to track their children or young children easily. This feature helps parents know where their children are, whether they are really safe or not, so that if there are any unusual cases, they can be handled.
Safety: After members share their location with each other, it will help more people know your location, and you will also know more people’s location. Rest assured that other than the people you share with, no one can see your real location on GPS.
CRASH DETECTION
This feature is more impressive because it detects incidents on the road where you or your loved ones travel. This is useful to help relatives, or you travel by another route or can help you report to the nearest medical facility for rescue.

Emergency response: When you are on the road, there will be situations such as the car’s light equipment breaking down or running out of gas, the application will immediately notify you.
Unresponsive alert: In cases where you are not in a collision, the application will also notify relatives and friends who are viewing your location. The purpose is to communicate that you are safe and that there is no need to worry about your current location.
Fast medical care: The application suggests to you the nearest medical or repair center so you can choose to fix the problem. Center address, operating hours, and repair center information are also fully provided so you can mobilize if there is a serious problem.
ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE
Smart application that provides necessary information of 24/7 rescue teams and the nearest service if you or your loved ones need it on the go. Not only you, but the people sharing your location are also provided with this information and they will help you contact them if you are having difficulty. Besides, you can also monitor your children and if there is a problem, you can contact the nearest support to ensure safety.

24/7 help: Contact the 24/7 rescue team immediately if you or your loved one has a problem. Besides, you can also quickly connect with them to get your questions answered and the rescue team to understand the current condition of you and your loved ones.
Towing: Besides incidents, you will also encounter cases such as running out of fuel, not being able to start the engine, etc., you can also contact the nearest gas station or support team to receive assistance. help.
Travel security: If the trip is long and you drive yourself, you can turn on the travel feature to go to different stations to have the staff there check the car to avoid problems on the way.
SOS ALERTS
The SOS signal in the application is always on the home page, and users can easily access it if they encounter an emergency or want to ask for help. Besides detecting abnormalities on the route, the application will also notify and support you so you can choose another route.

Emergency summons: The red SOS feature appears in the toolbar on the homepage for you to choose from if you need help. And the system quickly sends messages to the rescue team closest to your location.
Location sharing: You can text or send a voice message to the rescue team to let them know the current situation you or your loved ones are facing.
Life-threatening situations: Dangerous and emergency situations always appear in red so you can know to choose another route.
PLACE ALERTS
You can receive notifications from other members while sharing your real-time location. Many setting modes also appear for you to choose custom settings for schools, meeting rooms, etc. so as not to hinder your work.

Arrival notifications: You receive all important notifications from members and vice versa, members can also receive notifications when you send them.
Custom alerts: Set appropriate modes to facilitate the tracking process as well as other members sending notifications without disturbing you.
Location tracking: Tracks real-time location and can track the moving process to help you feel more secure about your members. Besides, tracking your children also becomes simpler and more convenient.
